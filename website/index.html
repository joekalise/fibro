About

Built for the complexity of fibromyalgia

Fibromyalgia doesn't behave like other conditions. Pain varies wildly day to day. Sleep quality, stress, diet, and activity all interact in ways that are hard to track in your head. Most health apps aren't built for this.

Fibro tracks your daily symptoms, spots patterns in your health data, and warns you when a flare looks like it's building. It connects brain fog to poor sleep, pain spikes to missed rest days, and mood dips to overexertion — things that only become visible when the data is in one place.

If you have fibromyalgia, I hope it helps you make sense of what your body is doing.
